Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from time tracking apps?

Time trackers show WHEN you work. TenaQ shows when you SHOULD work. We measure your mental energy and willpower capacity, not just hours logged.

Do I need an Apple Watch?

No, but it helps. The Watch adds HRV and other biometric data for better predictions. The iPhone alone provides full functionality.

What's this aMCC thing?

The anterior mid-cingulate cortex is the brain region that governs willpower and effort. Recent Stanford research shows it can be trained. TenaQ is the first app to apply this science practically.
